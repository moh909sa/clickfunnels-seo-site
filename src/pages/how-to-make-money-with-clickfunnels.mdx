## Strategy 1: E-commerce Funnels (High-Ticket Sales)

E-commerce funnels are one of the most profitable ways to use ClickFunnels. Here's how to set them up:

### Product Selection Criteria

- **High-Ticket Items**: $500+ products with good margins
- **Digital Products**: Courses, software, templates (100% profit margin)
- **Physical Products**: Premium items with strong value proposition
- **Services**: Consulting, coaching, done-for-you services

### Funnel Structure for Maximum Conversions

```
Landing Page → Lead Magnet → Sales Page → Upsell → Downsell → Thank You
```

**Landing Page**: Capture email addresses with valuable lead magnets
**Lead Magnet**: Free resource that demonstrates your expertise
**Sales Page**: Compelling product presentation with social proof
**Upsell**: Higher-priced product or service
**Downsell**: Lower-priced alternative if upsell is declined
**Thank You**: Confirmation and next steps

### Real Example: $2,000 Course Funnel

- **Lead Magnet**: "5-Step Sales Funnel Template" (free)
- **Main Product**: "Complete Funnel Mastery Course" ($2,000)
- **Upsell**: "1-on-1 Strategy Session" ($500)
- **Downsell**: "Funnel Templates Bundle" ($97)

**Potential Revenue per Customer**: $2,597
